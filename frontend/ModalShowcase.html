Design Patterns

Backgrounds

  • • Backdrop: bg-black/60 backdrop-blur-sm
  • • Modal: bg-dark-secondary
  • • Inputs: bg-dark-primary
  • • Cards: bg-dark-tertiary

Borders

  • • Default: border-dark-border
  • • Focus: border-accent
  • • Error: border-red-500
  • • Radius: rounded-xl (modal), rounded-lg (buttons)

Buttons

  • • Primary: bg-accent text-black
  • • Secondary: bg-dark-tertiary border-dark-border
  • • Danger: bg-red-500 text-white
  • • Ghost danger: bg-red-500/10 text-red-400
